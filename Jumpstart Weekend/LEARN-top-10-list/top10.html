Green Flash

West Coast IPA; Le Freak; we all know this is the hot beer of the season, in about three months another brewery will be the most popular, come on, it's San Diego, nothing lasts forever here. I really don't know what else to write, so I'm just going to continue going on and on, like Family Guy did in the beginning in those Star Wars episodes...you know. Honestly I kind of find that humor trite and pretentious in its cleverness, but it seems like that particular thing at the beginning in Star Wars style made people laugh...stupid people. And I'm stupid enough to laugh at it myself, so...you know what, this is about beer, and Chris thinks Green Flash is the best. You should check it out, really.
